About Us


At SkillSource, we partner with local volunteer organizations to bring you a range of
opportunities that match your interests and availability. From helping out at a local food
bank, to mentoring a young person, to building homes with a housing organization, there's
something for everyone.

Our platform is easy to use, whether you're looking to volunteer from your desktop or your
mobile device. You can search for opportunities by location, cause, and
time commitment, and apply to volunteer directly through the platform.

We believe that everyone has unique skills, passions, and interests that they can use to
make a positive impact in their communities. Whether you have a few hours a week to spare,
or you're looking to use your skills to support a cause you care about, SkillSource makes it
easy for you to get involved.

Vision & Mission

We seek a world of hope that is inclusive and just, where all people live in dignity and security
To save lives, enable social protection and defeat poverty through through local volunteer
organization with individuals looking to donate their time and skills to good cause
